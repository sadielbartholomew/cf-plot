Polar stereographic projections#

Example 4: North Pole polar stereographic projection contour plot#

Plotting a contour plot in the North Pole polar stereographic projection#
f = cf.read(f"cfplot_data/ggap.nc")[1]

cfp.mapset(proj="npstere")

cfp.con(f.subspace(pressure=500))

Example 5: South Pole polar projection contour plot with bounding latitude#

Changing the view area of a contour plot in the South Pole polar stereographic projection by setting the latitude limit to 30 degrees south#
f = cf.read(f"cfplot_data/ggap.nc")[1]

cfp.mapset(proj="spstere", boundinglat=-30, lon_0=180)

cfp.con(f.subspace(pressure=500))
